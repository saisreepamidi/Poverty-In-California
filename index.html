Across California Counties, Poverty Rate Varies

The nation's official poverty rate in 2011 was 15.0 percent, with 46.2 million people in poverty. Despite strong economic growth, the official poverty rate remains high throughout the country. California in particular faced an overall poverty rate of 16.4% in 2011. Some factors that affect economic development and poverty are fluctuations in migration, disease, under-employment, over population etc. have lead to an increase in poverty over the years.
